Audience

The target audience for this site will be Farmers in the Southeastern Idaho area.
This website will be helpful to farmers that would like to know if there are climate dangers to their crops or livestock.

Scenarios

This website will help solve the following scenarios:
1. Do I need to cover my tomatoes tonight to protect from frost?
2. Should I stop watering my crops?
3. What is the temperature for this week? Do I need to prepare my animals?
4. Can I get SMS alerts when frost or rain is expected?
5. What is the projected weather for the rest of the month? Can I get another harvest in?

Personas

Carl Stone:

Carl owns a large dairy farm. He is moderately wealthy and has access to the latest in technological innovations. He has several employees to help run his farm. He is always looking for ways to increase productivity. He knows that health of the livestock directly correlates to milk production. He wants to know ahead of time what the weather will be so he can ensure the health and well being of his livestock.

Wayne McCallister:

Wayne runs a small farm with a variety of crops. He inherited his family farm and his sons help him run it. His livelyhood is tied to the productivity of his potatoes. He doesn't have much money to invest in new techniques, so he is looking for things he can do to keep productivity high without spending a fortune. He would like to know if he can expect an early frost or a prolonged summer drought.
